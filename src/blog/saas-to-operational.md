SaaS products and operational platforms look similar from the outside — both are web apps with databases and APIs. But the moment you need a system to support real-time decisions under pressure, the architectural assumptions underneath a typical CRUD product start to crack.

I've built both. The transition from multi-tenant SaaS to operational software surfaces a set of tradeoffs that don't show up until you're in them.
