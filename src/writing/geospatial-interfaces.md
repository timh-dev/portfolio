Map interfaces for operational decisions are a different design problem than maps for exploration or consumer navigation. The people using them are under pressure, working with incomplete information, and need to act — not browse.

Building geospatial tools for wildfire operations forced a set of lessons that I haven't seen written down clearly anywhere else. Most GIS UI writing is about consumer maps. Operational maps are their own discipline.
